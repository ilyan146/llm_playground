## About Hugging Face

**Hugging Face** is a pioneering AI community and software development company, actively building the future of machine learning. Founded in 2016, it has grown into a vibrant platform where machine learning practitioners, researchers, and organizations collaborate to create and share models, datasets, and AI applications.

With a strong focus on democratizing accessible and effective machine learning, Hugging Face provides an open ecosystem to host and collaborate on over 1 million models and 250,000 datasets, spanning modalities including text, image, video, audio, and 3D.

Website: [huggingface.co](https://huggingface.co)

---

## What Hugging Face Offers

- **Models:** Access and contribute to a vast collection of open-source and enterprise-ready machine learning models.
- **Datasets:** Share and explore a large variety of datasets curated by the community and industry leaders.
- **Spaces:** Host and deploy ML-powered applications in an interactive environment directly on the platform.
- **Open Source Stack:** Tools and libraries that accelerate model development and deployment.
- **Enterprise Solutions:** Paid compute and custom support for businesses leveraging ML at scale.

*Join a community actively pushing the boundaries in AI, with trending models and applications updated continuously.*
