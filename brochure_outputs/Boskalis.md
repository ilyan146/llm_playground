## About Boskalis  
Boskalis is a globally operating company specializing in dredging and marine services. With a rich heritage and expertise, Boskalis is at the forefront of creating innovative maritime and infrastructural solutions that facilitate sustainable development and global connectivity.

---

## What We Do

### Activities  
Boskalis’ core activities revolve around two major domains:  

- **Dredging & Inland Infrastructure**  
  - Construction and maintenance of ports  
  - Coastal defense and riverbank protection  
  - Land reclamation  
  - Engineering and specialist dredging services

- **Offshore Energy**  
  - Offshore wind and oil & gas projects  
  - Marine surveys  
  - Seabed intervention  
  - Heavy marine transport and offshore heavy lifting  
  - Subsea cables and subsea services  
  - Salvage and towage operations  

---

## Markets Served

- **Ports**: Development, maintenance, and dedicated port services  
- **Offshore Energy**: Supporting the energy transition with offshore wind, oil & gas sectors  
- **Infrastructure**: Land reclamation, coastal protection, inland ports, environmental services, roads, railways, tunnels, and bridges

## Sustainability Commitment

Boskalis pursues a comprehensive sustainability strategy focused on:  

- Advancing the global energy transition  
- Creating innovative and climate-resilient infrastructure  
- Protecting marine ecosystems through climate adaptation and nature-based solutions  
- Managing environmental and social impacts responsibly, including emissions reduction and biodiversity conservation  
- Ensuring safety and responsible business practices  

The company follows a **Double Materiality Assessment** approach to align business growth with environmental and societal goals.

## Company Culture  

Boskalis fosters a culture of innovation, safety, and responsibility. The company values strong governance, ethical business practices, and leadership diversity. Its **Building with Nature** philosophy integrates natural processes into infrastructure projects for sustainable outcomes.
